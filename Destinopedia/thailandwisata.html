Wat Arun

Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is one of Bangkok's most iconic landmarks, famous for its stunning riverside location and soaring prang (Khmer-style tower) adorned with colorful porcelain. Located on the west bank of the Chao Phraya River, the temple dates back to the Ayutthaya period and is named after Aruna, the Indian god of dawn, symbolizing the rising sun.

Address: Ayasofya Meydanı, Sultanahmet, 34122 Fatih/Istanbul, Turkey.
Operational Hours: 09:00 - 19:00 (Depends on the season)
Price: Free (Visit the main part)
